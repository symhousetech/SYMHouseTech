Our social responsibility project. We will prepare a soup kitchen for the poor countries in Africa and we will meet the daily food needs of some of the people there. Another social aid project we will carry out is a financial responsibility project for people who have lost their homes and have no financial power for earthquake victims. As our project develops and becomes more popular, our financial power will increase and at the same rate, there will be an increase in the amount of money for the social responsibility project.
