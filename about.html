About

Our Social Responsibility Project We will prepare a soup kitchen for the poor countries of Africa and meet the daily food needs of some of the people there. Another social assistance project we will carry out is the financial responsibility project for earthquake victims who have lost their homes and have no financial means. In this project, we will provide psychological and financial support to people in very difficult situations, and we will meet their tent and shelter needs. As our project develops and becomes widespread, our financial strength will increase and the amount of money we will allocate to the social responsibility project will also increase.

Our foldable house project is made in a personalized way. Materials are added or removed and prepared according to the wishes of the person. There are sockets and solar panels to meet the need for electricity.
The system can be opened and closed with the Bluetooth system, and the most advantageous feature here is that the system can be opened and closed without human touch, so that a very comfortable use is possible, so that it can be used in the desired area and landscape and view.
